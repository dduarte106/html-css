WHAT IS

This is an assessment and comprehensive intervention of emotional safety measures applicable in the school routine.

WHO WHE ARE

We are professionals from the fields of technology, health, and education connected to a network of international experts committed to SUPPORTING and GUIDING young people and adults who seek to protect themselves and advocate for their peers against physical, emotional, digital, and sexual abuse.

GOAL

To bring this socio-emotional technology to the state of Rio de Janeiro, through a pilot project in Angra dos Reis. The program is being adapted for Brazil, and the expected outcomes include the development of the ability to:

  • Identify and avoid abusive relationships;
  • Understand the risks posed by abusive relationships;
  • Develop skills to maintain healthy relationships;
  • Know how to ask for help;
  • Instruct the community and participants to build a safe and sustainable environment.

Parceiros e Beneficiados

We are committed to results. To achieve this, we rely on the research of Dr. Jill Sharkey from the University of California, Santa Barbara, who, through the Gevirtz Institute, shares the results of her independent research in the United States.

These studies are being followed by the geologist and author of books on Self-Awareness, Vanessa Syrio, who founded Mangue Shrine - Healthy People, Safe Cities, inspired by the efforts of the Indian physician and social reformer, Maitreya Dadashreeji, founder of Maitribodh Parivaar, which shares the vision that we are One World and One Family.

The beneficiaries are all of us who will come together to build more educated, compassionate, healthy, and safe societies.
